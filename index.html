Our Story

Elevatex Fitness is your perfect destination to achieve your fitness goals. Established in Wellness City, we've been a part of the health and wellness community since 2021. Our vision is to provide an exceptional experience for every member, promoting personal improvement and supporting an active, healthy lifestyle.

Our Philosophy

To offer the best equipment, range of classes, knowledgeable staff, and personal advice in a welcoming environment.

We want to help our members go further, so if you have any suggestions about our clubs, classes, personal training sessions, or anything else, please come in and talk to one of our team members or if you prefer, drop us a note via our online enquiry forms.
